About Us

Phoenix Security Consultancy delivers practical, real-world security advisory and training services for organisations across Singapore. We specialise in SOP development, documentation systems, compliance audits, incident readiness, and operational security assessments. Our approach is grounded in clarity, professionalism, and operational realism, ensuring your security framework is both compliant and effective.

Our Services

  • Security Documentation & Assessment
    A comprehensive review of your security framework, including SOPs, post orders, incident reporting, patrol documentation, and compliance logs. We identify gaps, inconsistencies, and operational risks, then deliver a structured documentation pack that strengthens clarity, compliance, and day‑to‑day performance.
  • SOP Development & Compliance Audits
    We develop clear, practical Standard Operating Procedures tailored to your site’s unique operational environment. Our compliance audits ensure your security operations align with regulatory requirements, industry standards, and client expectations, reducing risk and improving accountability.
  • Incident Response Readiness
    We evaluate your organisation’s ability to respond to emergencies such as fire, medical incidents, threats, and evacuations. Through scenario planning, drills, and escalation mapping, we help you build a fast, coordinated, and confident response capability.
  • Security Training & Competency Advisory
    We provide skills‑based training and competency assessments for security officers, supervisors, and in‑house teams. Our training focuses on SOP adherence, incident handling, customer service, and operational discipline, ensuring your team is prepared, compliant, and effective.
  • QEHS Internal Audit Support
    We support organisations with internal audits aligned to ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 standards. Our QEHS advisory strengthens documentation accuracy, operational consistency, and audit readiness, helping you maintain a safe, compliant, and well‑structured workplace.
